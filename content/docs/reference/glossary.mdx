# Glossary

**Asset Agent** - the edge runtime closest to the equipment.

**Site Router** - the local coordination surface that turns policy and constraints into feasible actions.

**Portfolio Orchestrator** - the cloud coordination surface for policy distribution, proof ingest, and multi-site visibility.

**Proof Chain** - the signed record path that links intent, execution, and measured outcome.

**DepotOS** - the first commercial application of the JouleBridge platform for EV fleet depots.
