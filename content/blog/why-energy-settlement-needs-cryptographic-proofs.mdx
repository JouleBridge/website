# Why site coordination needs proof, not just dashboards

Dashboards help people see a site.

They do not automatically help people explain a site.

As distributed energy environments get denser, support, finance, and counterparties need stronger answers to three questions:

1. what did the site think was true
2. what action did it take
3. what happened next

That is why proof belongs in the control path, not in a reporting layer after the fact.
