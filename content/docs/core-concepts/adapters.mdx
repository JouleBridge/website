# Adapters

Adapters are where vendor-specific behavior first becomes site-readable.

## Current shape

The platform is designed to normalize inputs from surfaces such as:

- OCPP
- Modbus
- CAN
- SunSpec
- DLMS
- REST or webhook integrations

## Why adapters matter

Without a strong adapter layer, the site remains a collection of disconnected device stories.

The adapter boundary is therefore not just connectivity. It is the first trust and coordination boundary.
