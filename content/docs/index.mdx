# What is JouleBridge?

JouleBridge is the runtime, proof, and coordination layer for distributed energy.

The stack is designed around one practical requirement: a real site still needs to coordinate assets locally, and someone still needs a signed record of what happened after the fact.

The current public story is:

- JouleBridge is the platform
- DepotOS is the first commercial wedge for EV fleet depots
- the runtime split is Asset Agent, Site Router, Portfolio Orchestrator, and Console

## What the stack does

1. Reads site state from mixed-vendor assets and partner hardware.
2. Coordinates local actions against policy and safety limits.
3. Produces a signed proof chain for reservations, dispatches, acknowledgements, measurements, and exceptions.
4. Exposes portfolio and operator surfaces without moving the trust boundary away from the site.
