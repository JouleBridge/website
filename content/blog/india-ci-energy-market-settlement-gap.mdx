# Why JouleBridge starts with real sites, not every workflow at once

It is tempting to tell a broad story about every possible distributed energy workflow.

That is not how the product should be introduced.

The first wedges need to be:

- commercially painful
- technically differentiated
- repeatable on real hardware

EV fleet depots and industrial sites fit that bar better than trying to claim every commercial site, program, and utility path at once.
